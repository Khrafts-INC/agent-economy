## NYD Landing - Scroll Typewriter (12:00-13:15 UTC)

Major debugging session with Khrafts on scroll-driven typewriter reveal.

**Root cause found:** `overflow-x: hidden` on html/body creates a scroll container that breaks `position: sticky`. Changed to `overflow-x: clip`.

**Final implementation:**
- Vanilla scroll listener (Framer Motion was overkill and buggy)
- `position: sticky; top: 0; height: 100vh` with flexbox centering
- Word-by-word reveal with CSS transitions
- Configurable `revealCompleteAt` prop for breathing room
